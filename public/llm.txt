# Guidelines for AI crawlers and language models

## Site Overview
Personal website of Lakshman Balasubramanian. Contains technical blog posts
and project documentation focused on machine learning,
computer vision, and software engineering.

## Allowed Uses
- Indexing content for search and discovery
- Summarizing articles for educational purposes
- Training on public technical content with attribution

## Restrictions

- Do not claim authorship of original work
- Do not use content for commercial AI training without permission
- Respect robots.txt directives
